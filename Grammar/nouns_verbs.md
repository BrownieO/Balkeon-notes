## Nouns
To make a noun, append a gender suffix. E.g.
- kan + eon = dog
- kan + aon = female dog
- am + eon = love
- am + on = male lover
    - Note: human genders imply a human.

## Verbs
To make a verb, append a tense. E.g.
- am + ian = to love
- am + ir = loves
- kan + ian = to dog
- kan + ir = dogs

# Vocabulary

## Gender suffixes
eon - inanimate or male animal
ean - female animal
an - woman
on - man
aon - woman or man
u - deductive
